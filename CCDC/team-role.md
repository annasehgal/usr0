# Orange team
The purpose of orange team is to act as business / user front-facing, calling blue teams for injects, coustomer requirements for changes or fixes needed for a website to make sure it is accessible, and they are important to finish out on because they are worth quite a few points in the overall score.

# Blue team
- Defends network
- Does tasks provided by orange team, customer service points and task competition (as asked, in the correct way to match up expectation).
- Firewall rules
- Change default creds (including ones without password)
- Look for backdoors
- Wireshark open to check
- Know network topology
- Read the packet for questions asked possibly
- Finish injects by white teams/other injects, have a person who can write, not fully use ai, know what you are doing.
- Protect router first.
- Assign tasks early and know team strengths.
- Practice with previous year, and notice pcap files for practice.
- Write great reports including all screenshots of what you did, installed, not just commands, with full outputs.
